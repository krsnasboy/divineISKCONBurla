Volunteer Opportunities

Discover how you can contribute to the mission of ISKCON Burla

At ISKCON Burla, we believe that selfless service (seva) is a powerful spiritual practice that purifies the heart and brings us closer to Krishna. Our temple offers various opportunities for devotees to engage in meaningful service according to their skills, interests, and availability.

Whether you can spare a few hours a week or want to take on more regular responsibilities, your service is invaluable in maintaining the temple's daily operations and spreading the message of Lord Krishna.

Benefits of Volunteering

Service with love brings spiritual growth and divine blessings

Purification of Heart

Selfless service cleanses the heart and helps develop pure love for Krishna.

Spiritual Association

Connect with like-minded devotees and grow together in Krishna consciousness.

Practical Knowledge

Learn the practical application of bhakti-yoga through direct service.

Karma Reduction

Service burns away past karma and creates positive spiritual impressions.
