Important Information

Our temple store operates on a non-profit basis. All items are priced to cover costs only, with any surplus going toward temple maintenance and preaching activities.

Store Hours: Daily 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM (except festival days when open all day)

Location: Adjacent to the main temple hall

Note: We currently don't offer online ordering or shipping. Please visit the temple to make purchases.
